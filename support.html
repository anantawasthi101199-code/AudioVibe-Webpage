03

Account & Sign-In

If you cannot sign in, try the following first:

  • Confirm you have the latest version of AudioVibe installed
  • Use the "Forgot password" flow on the sign-in screen to reset your password
  • Check the email address you registered with — we send password reset links there
  • If you signed up with Google, use the same Google account to sign in

Still locked out? Email us with your registered email or username so we can verify your identity.

04

Audio & Playback

Audio not playing or sounding off?

  • Check your device volume and silent switch
  • Make sure AudioVibe has audio output permissions (Settings on your device)
  • Try switching between Wi-Fi and mobile data — slow networks can cause buffering
  • Restart the app fully (swipe it away from recent apps and reopen)

If the issue persists, tell us the audio title, your device model and OS version when you write in.

05

Uploading Audio

Uploads have file-size and duration limits to keep things smooth:

  • Audio cards and short clips: up to 5 minutes per file
  • Series episodes: up to 1 hour per file
  • Supported formats: standard audio (MP3, AAC, M4A, WAV) and video that we extract audio from

If an upload fails, double-check your file size and try again on a stable connection.

06

Notifications

Not getting notifications?

  • Allow notifications for AudioVibe in your device settings
  • Check that you haven't muted specific notification types in the in-app Notifications settings
  • For background delivery, ensure battery optimisation isn't restricting AudioVibe
07

Delete Your Account

You can request deletion of your AudioVibe account and all associated data at any time.

Email generalinquiries@audiovibe.co from your registered email address with the subject line "Delete my account". We will confirm the request and complete deletion within 30 days, in line with applicable law.

08

Report a Bug

When reporting a bug, please include:

  • What you were doing when it happened (steps to reproduce)
  • What you expected vs. what actually happened
  • Your device model and OS version (e.g., iPhone 13, iOS 18.4)
  • The app version (Settings → About in AudioVibe)
  • A screenshot or screen recording if possible

Send to the platform-specific address above so we can route it to the right engineer.
